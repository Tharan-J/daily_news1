Visit to BIT by PSGR Krishnammal College for Women to Explore Skill Development and Innovation
A delegation from PSGR Krishnammal College for Women, Coimbatore, visited Bannari Amman Institute of Technology on May 05, 2025, to explore the institution’s skill development and innovation initiatives. The delegation included Dr. Yesodha Devi N (Secretary), Dr. Harathi P B (Principal), Dr. Bala Vijayalakshmi (Head-Physics), Dr. Nalini (Head-Chemistry), Dr. Karpagavalli (Head-Computer Science), Dr. Shanthi (Dean-Costume Design & Fashion), Dr. Nirmal Kumar (Head-Biotechnology), Dr. Ramesh Subramani (Dean-Research), Dr. Priyadarshini (IIC & CIIED Coordinator), and Dr. Chithra (BIRAC Coordinator).
